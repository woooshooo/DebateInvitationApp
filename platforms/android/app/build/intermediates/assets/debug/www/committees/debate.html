GENERAL INFO

The Ateneo Debate Varsity (ADV) is the official debate and public speaking organization of the Ateneo de Davao University. The ADV is one of the most competitive debate institutions in the region and in the country. It is also active in its thrust to promote debate in schools within Davao City as well as neighboring cities.

MISSION

The Ateneo Debate Varsity aims to be a nationally competitive debate organization through continuous quality training and recruitment across student generations. It also seeks to be socially responsible through the holistic development of its members.
